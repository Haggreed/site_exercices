ASSASSINATION CLASSROOM

Title : Assassination Classroom
Writer : FLIPFLOPS
Years : 2012

Synopsis: In a very ordinary high school there is a strange class. A class of outcasts who have been left behind and become murderers in the making in order to accomplish a mission, to get rid of their new teacher. A lunatic octopus has decided to blow up the Earth. However, he seems immortal. He even enjoys watching others trying to kill him, so how will they kill him before the Earth explodes? Why did this strange creature choose to come and teach?

Assassination Classroom (暗殺教室) is a manga created in 2012 by Yûsei MATSUI. Assassination Classroom is classified in the Shonen category and was first pre-published in Weekly Shônen Jump magazine. The work is published in France by Kana and has 21 volumes in all (finished in Japan).
