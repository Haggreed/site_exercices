That Time I Got Reincarnated as a Slime

Title : ONE PIECE
Writer : FLIPFLOPS
Years : 2012

Synopsis: Minami Satoru was living a normal life until the day he was stabbed by a criminal which put an end to his life after 37 years... or at least, that's what should have happened. But he suddenly found himself unable to see and hear ... In this kind of state, he became aware of the truth: he had been reincarnated as Slime !!!!

Me, when I reincarnate in slime (転生したらスライムだった件) is a manga created in 2015 by Taiki KAWAKAMI, FUSE and MITZ VAH. Moi, quand je me reincarne en slime is classified in the category Seinen and was first pre-published in the magazine Shonen Sirius. The work is edited in France by Kurokawa and has 10 volumes to date (currently being published in Japan with 13 volumes). Volume 11 of Me, when I reincarnate in slime is scheduled for Thursday, April 9, 2020.
