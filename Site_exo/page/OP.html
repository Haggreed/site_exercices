ONE-PIECE

Title : ONE PIECE
Writer : FLIPFLOPS
Years : 2012

Synopsis: We are in the age of pirates. Luffy, a mischievous boy, dreams of becoming the king of pirates by finding the "One Piece", a fabulous treasure. One day, Luffy accidentally swallowed an evil fruit that turned him into a rubber man. Since then, he has been able to contort his elastic body in all directions, but he has lost the ability to swim. With the help of his precious friends, he will have to face fearsome pirates in ever more incredible adventures.

One Piece (ワンピース) is a manga created in 1997 by Eiichiro ODA. One Piece is classified in the Shonen category and was first pre-published in the Weekly Shônen Jump magazine. The work is published in France by Glénat Manga and has 93 volumes to date (currently being published in Japan with 95 volumes). Volume 94 of One Piece is scheduled for Wednesday, April 1, 2020.
